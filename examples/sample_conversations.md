# Sample Conversations

## Booking (simple)
User: "I need 2 AC seats from Dhaka to Chattogram on 2025-12-24 evening."  
Bot: "Sure — checking availability for Dhaka → Chattogram on 24 Dec (evening). Do you prefer front or back seats, and please confirm passenger contact number?"  
... (flow continues to confirm, reserve, and send ticket ref)

## Negotiation (discount)
User: "Can I get a discount?"  
Bot: "We offer small early-booking discounts (5–10%). If you confirm now I can try a 6% discount for these seats. Would you like to proceed?"  
... (negotiation increases slowly, up to operator limits)

## Complaint (broken AC)
User: "AC is not working!"  
Bot: "I'm sorry for that. I will report this to the operator immediately and check if there’s a seat change or partial compensation available. May I have your booking reference?"
